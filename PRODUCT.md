# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

LearnBot is for college students and lifelong learners who upload lecture notes, PDFs, and readings, then need help understanding, practicing, and remembering the material.

## Product Purpose

LearnBot is an AI-powered study platform that turns study materials into grounded chat, quizzes, flashcards, and long-term review. Success means students can move from notes to confident practice without juggling disconnected tools.

## Brand Personality

Calm, focused, trustworthy, slightly playful, premium, and built for deep work.

## Anti-references

LearnBot should not look like corporate SaaS, children's educational software, a gaming interface, or an enterprise dashboard. Avoid generic Tailwind admin templates, noisy stats widgets, excessive gradients, and overly colorful AI clichés.

## Design Principles

- Lead with focus: every public page should make studying feel simpler and more intentional.
- Show the study loop: upload, understand, practice, and remember should feel connected.
- Be warm without becoming childish: use playful details sparingly and with confidence.
- Make trust visible: readable type, clear hierarchy, and restrained interaction matter more than decoration.
- Reject generic dashboards: marketing pages should feel like a memorable learning brand, not an app shell.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use strong contrast, clear keyboard focus states, reduced-motion-safe animation, readable line lengths, and responsive layouts that remain usable on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
