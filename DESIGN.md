# Design

## Visual Personality

Warm, calm, intelligent, and memorable. LearnBot should feel like a beautiful study desk, premium note-taking software, and an AI tutor sitting beside the learner.

## Color System

- Background: `#FAF7F2`
- Card / surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Primary text: `#1F1B2D`
- Secondary text: `#6F677A`
- Border: `#E8DED4`
- Primary accent: `#8B5CF6`
- Secondary accent: `#D97745`
- Support accent: `#8FAF9C`
- Dark CTA: `#111827`

For the public landing page, the current visual reference expands this into a pale lemon editorial palette with black type, black pill navigation, black CTA buttons, simple dot motifs, and occasional pale blue sections.

## Typography

Use a clean sans-serif stack with strong weight contrast. Public marketing pages may use oversized confident headings, but keep line breaks controlled and avoid cramped letter spacing.

## Components

- Cards use soft elevation, subtle borders, and calm spacing.
- Buttons are rounded, confident, and slightly elevated.
- Inputs use warm borders and soft focus rings.
- Icons use Lucide outlined style with consistent stroke width.

## Motion

Motion should be subtle, smooth, and calm. Use fade-up, gentle scale, and hover lift. Avoid bounce, flashy transitions, and excessive movement.

## Spacing

Use generous whitespace. Never crowd cards. Let content breathe, especially on marketing pages.

## Overall Feel

70% professional, 20% playful, 10% magical AI.
